Privacy Policy — YieldGuard
Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Who we are
DoroqTech (“we”, “us”) provides the YieldGuard mobile application (“App”) for managing shop operations (e.g. products, sales, waste, reports). Contact: doroqtech@gmail.com. Website: https://github.com/DTTOPRAK/yieldguard-docs.

2. Scope
This policy describes how we handle information in connection with the App. It applies to users of the App on Android (and other platforms if offered).

3. Information you provide
You may enter business-related data in the App (e.g. product names, categories, prices, quantities, sales, waste, expenses, notes). This data is stored locally on your device in an on-device database (SQLite) unless you choose to export or share it.

4. Local storage and preferences
The App stores operational data and certain preferences on your device (including language and app settings, and flags such as whether onboarding was completed). We do not operate a mandatory cloud account in the App for this core data; we do not receive your business database by default simply because you use the App.

5. Purchases and subscriptions
If you subscribe to YieldGuard PRO, Google Play processes payments. We use RevenueCat to validate subscriptions and unlock features. RevenueCat and Google may process identifiers related to purchases according to their own policies:

6. Notifications
If you enable notifications, the operating system may show reminders you configure in the App. Notification permission is requested only where required by the platform.

7. Exports and files
When you export reports (e.g. spreadsheets or PDFs) or save files, data is written where you choose on your device or to apps you open the file with. You are responsible for copies you create outside the App.

8. Advertising
The free tier may show in-app placeholders promoting YieldGuard PRO. The current build does not integrate a third-party advertising SDK to collect data for personalized ads; if that changes, we will update this policy.

9. Analytics
We do not use third-party analytics SDKs in the App as described in this policy. If we add analytics, we will update this policy and, where required, obtain consent.

10. Security
We rely on your device security (lock screen, OS updates). Because primary data stays on the device, loss of the device or uninstalling the App without backup may permanently delete your data unless you have exported a backup.

11. Children
The App is intended for business users. It is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country).

12. Your rights
Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR, KVKK), you may have rights to access, correction, deletion, or objection regarding personal data we process. Much of your operational data is under your control on the device; you can delete it by deleting App data or uninstalling. For subscription-related data held by Google/RevenueCat, use their tools and privacy settings.

13. International transfers
RevenueCat and Google may process data in countries outside yours. Their policies describe safeguards.

14. Changes
We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change; continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy where permitted by law.
